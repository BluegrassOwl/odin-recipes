Appalacian Soup Beans

Warm and Comforting

This ham and bean soup is incredibly filling and satisfying. However, if you'd like it to be a little thicker, try adding a cornstarch slurry — or use this unlikely ingredient, BLENDED FRENCH FRIES!.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water
  • 1 pound dry great northern beans, sorted and rinsed
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • ½ stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 ham hock
  • 2 cups chopped ham
  • ½ teaspoon ground white pepper, or to taste

How to Make

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place water and beans in a large pot; bring to a boil over high heat. Stir in salt and remove the pot from heat; cover, and let stand for 1 hour.
  3. Add carrots, onion, celery, garlic, mustard, and bay leaves to the pot with beans; stir well. Add ham hock and bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 hour.
  4. Remove ham hock and discard. Stir in chopped ham and simmer for 30 minutes. Season with ground white pepper to taste.
